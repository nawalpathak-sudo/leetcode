# AICTE BOS Reference — B.Tech Program Framework

## Program Structure

- **Duration:** 4 years / 8 semesters
- **Total Credits:** 160
- **Credits per Semester:** ~20 (flexible)
- **Semester Duration:** 22 weeks (90 instructional days)

## Credit System (L-T-P)

Each subject is defined by an **L-T-P** pattern:

| Component | Meaning | Credit Conversion |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| **L** (Lecture) | Classroom theory | 1 hour/week = 1 credit |
| **T** (Tutorial) | Problem-solving, discussions | 1 hour/week = 1 credit |
| **P** (Practical) | Lab, hands-on | 2 hours/week = 1 credit |

**Examples:**
- `3-1-0` → 3L + 1T + 0P = **4 credits**, 4 contact hours/week
- `3-0-2` → 3L + 0T + 2P = **4 credits**, 5 contact hours/week (but 2 lab hours count as 1 credit)
- `0-0-4` → Pure lab = **2 credits**, 4 contact hours/week

**Formulas:**
- Theory Credits = L + T
- Practical Credits = P ÷ 2
- Total Credits = L + T + (P ÷ 2)
- Contact Hours/week = L + T + P

| Code | Category | Typical Credits | Purpose |
|------|----------|----------------|---------|
| HSS / HSMC | Humanities & Social Sciences | ~12 | Communication, ethics, management |

**Total: 160 credits across ~52 courses**

## Semester Credit Limits

- Minimum: 16 credits/semester
- Maximum: 26 credits/semester
- Recommended: 20 credits/semester

## Weekly Structure (Typical)

For a 20-credit semester with 5 working days:
- ~25-30 contact hours/week
- ~5-6 hours/day of scheduled classes
- Includes lectures, tutorials, and lab sessions

## How BOS Defines a Week

A BOS for a given semester lists all subjects with their L-T-P. The sum of all contact hours tells you how a student's week looks:

| Subject | L-T-P | Credits | Hours/Week |
|---------|-------|---------|------------|
| Data Structures | 3-1-0 | 4 | 4 |
| DS Lab | 0-0-4 | 2 | 4 |
| Discrete Math | 3-1-0 | 4 | 4 |
| OS | 3-0-0 | 3 | 3 |
| OS Lab | 0-0-2 | 1 | 2 |
| Communication | 2-0-0 | 2 | 2 |
| Elective | 3-0-0 | 3 | 3 |
| **Total** | | **19** | **22** |

## Internship

- 6-8 weeks mandatory
- Typically summer after 2nd or 4th semester
- Evaluated and credited in subsequent semester

## NEP 2020 Additions

- Multi-disciplinary minor: 11 credits
- Honors pathway: additional credits beyond 160
- Academic Bank of Credits (cross-institution transfer)
- Multiple exit: Certificate (1yr), Diploma (2yr), Degree (3yr), B.Tech (4yr)
